Microsite: Creative Director Role

I creative directed this website with my designer and developer, Aiden Cole. The site explores the history of jungle music, drawing from the music's electronic aesthetics in its image, color, and typeface choices.

Microsite: Designer/Developer Role

I designed and developed this microsite in collaboration with Claire Cheung, my creative director. She asked that I make a site about the history of Chanel and draw from their simple, monochromatic aesthetics. I aimed for ease of navigation and a minimalist interface.
